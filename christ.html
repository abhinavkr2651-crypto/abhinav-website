Christ the Redeemer

Introduction

Christ the Redeemer is a colossal statue of Jesus Christ located in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. It stands atop Mount Corcovado and overlooks the city.

Construction

Construction began in 1922 and was completed in 1931. It was designed by Brazilian engineer Heitor da Silva Costa.

  • Height: 30 meters.
  • Material: Reinforced concrete and soapstone.
  • Art Deco architectural style.

Symbolism

The statue symbolizes peace, Christianity, and Brazilian culture. It is one of the most recognized monuments in the world.
